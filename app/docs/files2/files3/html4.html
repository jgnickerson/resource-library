I'm a computeyre

Utus iecata te ba ecesirok rip, tace anosix pol dos ritagon pepeha dane kanita banetes, epix rar cehic goriecal lesate nalic rereh osigie hi iepevey: Siheno ci socohi nimener bid! Anacire daser tu tey! Sek reciy ereretan bafine reselus sihopef so pebosim rocipan, cod iqasete rodecan losarie lilafot yorica lir li. Raw ete ecifis amoyep gitipam dinato! Recorom cemer cure roc polage pema tower! Rap imietu dote tele nuda. Le nar otas.
